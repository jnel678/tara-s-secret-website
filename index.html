Privacy & security

Tara's Secret respects your privacy. When you place an order, our payment partner collects information such as your name, email address and delivery details so your order can be processed and shipped.

Payments are processed securely by a third-party payment provider (such as Paystack or Flutterwave). Your card or bank details are handled and stored by them using their own encryption and security controls. Tara's Secret does not store your full card number or CVV on this website.

We may use your email address to send you order updates and, if you agree, news about Tara's Secret products and offers. You can ask us to update or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at tarassecrete@gmail.com.
